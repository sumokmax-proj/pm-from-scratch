3 years as a PM, less than a year since getting my PMP.

I'm not writing this as an expert — I'm writing to learn by documenting. When I first started as a PM, I was completely lost. There were very few practical PM resources in Korean, and the ones that existed were too theoretical to apply in real work.

I started this blog hoping it would help others who were just as confused as I was.
