Hi, I'm PM From Scratch.

3 years as a PM, and less than a year since getting my PMP.
I'm not starting this blog because I'm an expert. I'm starting it **to learn by documenting**.

## Honestly Speaking

When I first started as a PM, I was completely lost.

> "It's just scheduling meetings and managing timelines, right?"

That's what I thought at first. Reality was very different.

- Why is managing stakeholder expectations so hard?
- Where do I even start with a WBS?
- What do I do when a team member keeps missing deadlines?
- What happens when a client suddenly changes requirements?

I'm writing this blog to document my journey of finding answers to these questions.

## What I'll Cover

**📚 PM Fundamentals**
